Definition

A basic privacy policy outlines your website’s relationship with users’ personal information. To succeed online and avoid legal turmoil, your website needs a privacy policy agreement. The first step to creating a compliant and comprehensive privacy policy is understanding exactly what that is. A privacy policy is a legal document that informs your site’s users about how you collect and handle their personal information.
